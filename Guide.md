# DevTools Quick Guide

1. Open the page and press F12 to open DevTools.

![Step 1](lessons_files/Step%201.png)

2. Click the element picker icon and select the target element.

![Step 2](lessons_files/Step%202.png)

3. Right-click the highlighted node.

![Step 3](lessons_files/Step%203.png)

4. Copy the selector or XPath you need.

![Step 4](lessons_files/Step%204.png)
